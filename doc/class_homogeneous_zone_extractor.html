Returns the indices of the rectangles that intersects a particular rectangle. The intersection relationship is controlled by a threshold, which operates on the intersection rate, which is computed by dividing the area of the intersection by the area of the intersecting rectangle. When this threshold equals 1, this method tests the complete inclusion of the rectangle in the others.
