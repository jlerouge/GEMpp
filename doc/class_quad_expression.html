QHash<Quad, double> QuadExpression::quadTerms_
private

The quadratic terms of the QuadExpression, stored as key-value pairs : the Quad is the key whereas the coefficient is the value. Hence, a Quad may be present at most one time in the terms, but its coefficient may be updated.
